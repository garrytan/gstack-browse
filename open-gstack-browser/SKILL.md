## Step 0: Pre-flight cleanup

```bash

  _OLD_PID=$(cat "$(git rev-parse --show-toplevel)/.gstack/browse.json" 2>/dev/null | grep -o '"pid":[0-9]*' | grep -o '[0-9]*')
```
